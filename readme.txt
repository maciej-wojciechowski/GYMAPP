Simple app to track your progress at the gym.

1.Signup and Login
2.Add Training in New Training
3.You can edit/delete your trainings in History (click on training)
4.You can see your stats from each session in stats (weight * reps * sets = value in chart)

Techstack:

-VANILLA JS
-SCSS
-FIREBASE
-CHART JS
